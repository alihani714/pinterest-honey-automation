This privacy policy describes how Practical Wisdom Press ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Pinterest application.

- **Pinterest Account Data**: When you authorize our app, we access your Pinterest profile information and posting permissions to help you publish content about honey and natural health.

## How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
